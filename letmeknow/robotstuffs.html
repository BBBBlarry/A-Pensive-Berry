Homework

  • Make a program using three arrays that load your families' names (first and last) and ages, up to 25 family members.

  • If you feel thug: Make a loop where you ask for the names instead, unless you enter the word "done", you stop entering names.

  • Print out how many members you entered.

  • Ask for an index, and print the information at that index.

  • Format: Last First, Age.
